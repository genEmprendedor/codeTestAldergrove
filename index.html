about

Aldergrove Housing Co-operative Ltd (AHC) was completed for occupancy in 1982. We are a member owned; non-profit housing project, governed by a democratically elected board. Aldergrove Housing Co-operative Ltd. consists of 34 units, ranging in size from two-bedroom units to four-bedroom units.
